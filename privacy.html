GigProfit

Finance Tracker for Gig Workers

Last updated: June 6, 2026

GigProfit ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by MoneyTrack Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app at gigprofitapp.github.io/GigProfit.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Financial data — income entries, expense records, and mileage logs that you manually enter
  • Usage data — basic app usage information to improve performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the GigProfit service
  • To sync your data across devices via our secure cloud backend (Supabase)
  • To calculate profit estimates, tax estimates, and mileage deductions
  • To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data.

4. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We do not receive your Google password or access to your Google account beyond basic profile information. Your use of Google Sign-In is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your financial data is yours — we never share it with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at gigprofitapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

GigProfit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: gigprofitapp@gmail.com
